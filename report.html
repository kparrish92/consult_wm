RQ1: Does tDCS enhance listening and reading comprehension?

It does for listening, but not reading.

Listening

Based on a sample of 15 per group, power level of .8 and alpha of .05, the minimum detectable effect was d = 1.06.

The data showed a small descriptive effect for the control group going from pretest to posttest (d = 0.21). The power of this comparison was low: 0.09. To achieve a statistically powered sample, 366 participants per group would have been needed.

For the treatment group, the effect was large (d = 1.34. This comparison was statistically powered: 0.94. The minimum number of participants in this case to achieve a statistically powered sample, 10 participants per group would have been needed.
